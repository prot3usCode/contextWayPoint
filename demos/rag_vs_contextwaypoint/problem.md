# Problem

An operations analyst is investigating a concrete question:

Why has order 4 not shipped?

Known symptoms:

- the order exists
- the customer expects it to ship already
- the answer could involve payment, inventory, shipment workflow, or explanation rules

The question is not only "what chunks are relevant?"

The question is also:

1. what context should come first?
2. what evidence should come next?
3. how should the explanation be assembled at the end?

This is the kind of problem where retrieval helps, but route definition matters.
