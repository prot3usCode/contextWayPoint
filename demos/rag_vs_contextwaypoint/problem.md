# Problem

An operations analyst is investigating an order that looks stuck in fulfillment.

Known symptoms:

- the order exists
- the customer expects it to ship already
- there may be a payment issue, an inventory issue, or a shipment workflow issue

The question is not just "what chunks are relevant?"

The question is:

1. what context should come first?
2. what evidence should come next?
3. how should the explanation be assembled at the end?

This is the kind of problem where retrieval helps, but route definition matters.
